## Overview

Agenda Professional Suite is a complete project and meeting note platform that combines timeline-based organization with task tracking and cross-note linking for ongoing professional work.

On-the-record and draft note states distinguish finalized notes from work in progress, keeping a clear record of what has been decided versus what is still being worked through.

Linked notes and category overviews connect related entries across different projects and dates, surfacing the full history relevant to any topic regardless of when each note was written.

Note templates for recurring meeting types — standups, reviews, one-on-ones — standardize repeated documentation, reducing the setup needed each time a similar meeting occurs.

On-this-day style review surfaces notes from the same date in previous months or years, providing useful historical context for recurring meetings and ongoing projects without manual searching.

iOS and iPad companion apps keep the same timeline-based notes accessible on every device, syncing automatically so that a note started in a meeting on the iPad is immediately available on the Mac.

Agenda Professional Suite is the platform for professionals who need their notes to reflect the actual chronology of their work, with the organization to make that history genuinely useful later.

## Performance & Integration

Agenda is engineered around macOS technologies that handle performance-critical work efficiently. Tasks run in the background without slowing down the rest of your system — you can keep working while Agenda processes operations quietly behind the scenes.

The app integrates deeply with the macOS ecosystem out of the box:

- Launch workflows directly through **Spotlight**
- Preview files instantly with **Quick Look**
- Trigger actions from any app via the **Services menu**
- Fully compatible with **Shortcuts** for automated workflows
